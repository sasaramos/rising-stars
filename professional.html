Resume

A resume, or holistic view of one's professional background, is key in landing a job interview. A complete resume includes:

  • Contact Information
  • Prior Experience
  • Skills/Achievements

Employers may also request verification from references: people who are familiar with your work habits, skills and strengths.

Interview

A job interview allows an employer to become familiar with you as a person. Here are some tips from the experts:

  • Demonstrate punctuality: Early is "on time".
  • Confidence is key: Anxiety before an interview is natural - try to soothe yourself with breathing exercises.
  • Be yourself: You have limited time to showcase your personality and strengths - use it wisely.
  • Follow-up: Send a "thank you" email to your interviewer within 24 hours following your interview.

An interview does not guarantee your employment; it allows you to get your foot in the door.

Network

Expanding your network opens up opportunities: scholarships, internships and job offers. Connections can be made through:

  • Social media platforms
  • Networking events
  • Conversations with teachers, counselors and mentors

LinkedIn is a common networking platform used by students and professionals alike. In the digital age, your online presence can be cruicial in applying for a job.
